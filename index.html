SPECI vs METAR — Quick Rule
METARRoutine — issued every 30 min at :00 and :30 UTC
SPECIImmediate — issued as soon as any criterion above is triggered. Not time-bound. Next routine METAR cancels the SPECI status
TrendAfter a SPECI, continue to monitor — issue additional SPECIs if further threshold crossings occur
